Lasagna

A dish of lasagna

This is the greatest lasagna on planet earth. It will make your tongue jump for joy.

The recipe comes from my grandma, the greatest Bohemian chef to emigrate to the U.S.

Ingredients
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Hamburger
  • Rutabagas

Steps

  2. Bake in oven at 425 degrees
  3. Eat
